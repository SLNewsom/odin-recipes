Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a fantastic dish that combines pasta along with tomato sauce and cheese. It is a classic Italian dish that is loved by many people around the world. The recipe is quite simple and can be made by anyone.

The ingredients required for making lasagna are pasta, cheese, tomato sauce, and meat. The meat can be either beef or chicken. The pasta is boiled and then layered with tomato sauce, cheese, and meat. The layers are then baked in an oven until the cheese melts and the dish is ready to be served.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Prepare the tomato sauce
  4. Layer the pasta with tomato sauce, cheese, and meat
  5. Bake in the oven
  6. Serve hot
