Chili Recipe

Chili is a delicious and hearty dish that is perfect for a cold winter day or a casual dinner with friends. It is made by cooking ground beef with beans, tomatoes, and spices until it is thick and flavorful. Chili is often served with toppings such as cheese, sour cream, and green onions. It is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste preferences.

The ingredients, which may be altered to taste, for making chili are ground beef, beans, tomatoes, onions, garlic, chili powder, cumin, and salt. The ground beef is cooked with onions and garlic until it is browned. The beans, tomatoes, and spices are then added to the pot and simmered until the chili is thick and flavorful. Chili can be made on the stovetop or in a slow cooker for added convenience.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Chili Powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef with onions and garlic
  2. Add the beans, tomatoes, and spices
  3. Simmer until the chili is thick and flavorful
  4. Serve hot with toppings
